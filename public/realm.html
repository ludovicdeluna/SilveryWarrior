• Titan Champion Purchase all Titan Upgrades.
    • Shows once you’ve unlocked the Titans faction (get to 1B gems, buy 20 excavations, have at least 300 Hall of Legends in a game, buy the Titan Fragment for 1No [1e30] Coins and 25,000 each of Angel and Goblin Faction Coins).
    • Unlock: Titan Heritage. Increases Royal Exchange production multiplier by an additive 15% (so from 10% to 25%).
  • Druid Champion Purchase all Druid Upgrades.

  • Faceless Champion Purchase all Faceless Upgrades.

    • Unlock: Dwarven Heritage. Increases production of all buildings based on the number of excavations you’ve completed.
